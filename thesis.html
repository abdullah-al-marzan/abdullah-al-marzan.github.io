Thesis Presentation

My thesis focused on identifying potential biomarkers and therapeutic targets within the molecular landscape of PROM. It utilized RNA-seq data and multiple bioinformatics pipelines to analyze gene expression signatures and pathway enrichment.

Presentation Slides

Highlights

  • Advanced DEG analysis with DESeq2 and edgeR
  • Construction of co-expression and PPI networks
  • Identification of miRNA-mRNA regulatory axes
  • Validation through literature mining and databases

Research Outcome

This research contributed to the understanding of PROM at a molecular level and was published in a peer-reviewed journal. It has also been presented at international conferences.
